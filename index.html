So Far


Under the leadership of Curtis Brown, Brown Stub Productions is committed to developing captivating musical works for the stage and screen.


Whether your narrative is told through text or song (or both!) we believe it deserves to be told the best way possible. Established in 2019, Brown Stub Productions is thrilled to unveil its premiere production of "Before They Were Them: a musical short film.”


Brown Stub Productions is currently looking for new work to produce. We believe telling stories is a worthy and important endeavour. Please visit the Submissions page for more details!

Curtis Brown, Producer


A Toronto based producer and founder of Brown Stub Productions. He has helped develop musicals like Come From Away & The Theory of Relativity with the Canadian Music Theatre Project, The Girl Who with Noisemaker (UK) , Bloodless: The Trail of Burke and Hare with Theatre 20 and Columbus: The Musical with Royal Caribbean Cruise Lines.


Curtis' love for capturing stories on screen, along with his life-long passion for musical theatre, led him to produce “Before They Were Them” which premiered at the TIFF Lightbox in November 2019. He holds a Master of Arts Degree from the Royal Conservatoire of Scotland and an Advanced Diploma from Sheridan College.


Along with his education, Curtis’ experience within the entertainment industry working with leading Broadway and West end creatives has helped him garner the skills to develop and produce meaningful, respected and well-crafted work.
